Sally Finocchario is a creative living in Rochester, New York. Her earliest memories are of art. She grew up in an extremely creative environment; her mother and uncle being her biggest influences. She learned how to draw, paint, sew, cook and create at a very young age. Sally started her career working at print companies as a typesetter and graphic designer.


After her three children, Sally was inspired to go back to school and get certified in art education. She worked as a secondary digital media art teacher for fourteen years. In 2016 Sally resigned from teaching to pursue her first love and passion; graphic design. As a professional with an excellent record of success, she is seeking a challenging opportunity to design where her skills, education, training and many years of experience can be fully utilized. Sally is a self motivated, high energy leader, who takes iniative and gets the job done.
